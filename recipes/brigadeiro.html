Brigadeiro

Description

Brigadeiro (bree-gah-day-ro) is one of the most classic Brazilian desserts.

This simple dessert is made with just a handful of ingredients, mainly condensed milk and chocolate powder.

Ingredients:
  • Sweet condensed milk
  • Chocolate powder
  • Sprinkles
  • Butter
Steps:
  1. Combine all ingredients in a saucepan and cook over medium heat
  2. Stir and scrape over and over.
  3. Let it cool.
  4. Roll into balls
