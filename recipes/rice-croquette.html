Rice Croquette

Description

This is a classic pub appetizer, very popular and spread in the south-east region of Brazil.

It is a simple and cheap dish that use use up leftovers.

Ingredients:

  • 300g leftover cold white rice
  • 40g melted butter
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp chopped parsley
  • ...

Steps:

  1. Mix all ingredients together
  2. Add milk gradually
  3. Make the shapes into balls
  4. Pan fry
